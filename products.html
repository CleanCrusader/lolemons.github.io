Quick reference

What you can clean with it

Grease & countertops

A few sprays of the ready-to-use formula cuts through cooking grease without residue.

Soap scum & bathroom

D-limonene dissolves soap scum on tile, shower glass, and fixtures without harsh chemicals.

Hardwood & tile

Dilute the concentrate 10:1 for a gentle floor cleaner that leaves a fresh scent, not a waxy film.

Air freshener

Add concentrate to a diffuser or ultrasonic mister. Real oil diffuses differently — richer, cleaner than synthetic.

Wash booster

A capful of concentrate in the laundry rinse cycle adds freshness to towels and pet bedding.

Pet accidents

The enzyme formula works on urine, vomit, and feces on carpet, upholstery, or hardwood — not just masks it.
