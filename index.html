Frequently Asked Questions

Is this product safe to use around pets and children?

Yes! Our lemon oil formula uses natural ingredients that are safe for use around pets and children when used as directed. The natural citrus enzymes target odors without harmful chemicals.

Will this work on old, set-in stains and odors?

Absolutely! Our powerful formula is designed to penetrate deep into surfaces to break down even old, dried urine and other pet stains that other products can't eliminate.

How much product should I use?

For fresh stains, saturate the area completely. For set-in odors, you may need multiple applications. Our concentrate can be diluted for lighter applications or used at full strength for tough problems.

What surfaces can I use this on?

Our formula is safe for use on carpets, rugs, upholstery, hardwood floors, tile, concrete, pet bedding, clothing, and most other surfaces. Always test on a small, inconspicuous area first.
