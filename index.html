Frequently Asked Questions

Is your lemon oil safe for all surfaces?

Our lemon oil is safe for most household surfaces, but we recommend testing on an inconspicuous area first. It's best to avoid using concentrated oil on untreated or unsealed wood, as well as some plastics.

How long does the scent last after cleaning?

The fresh lemon scent typically lasts 24-48 hours after application, leaving your home with a natural citrus freshness without being overwhelming.

How is cold-pressed lemon oil different from other lemon cleaners?

Cold-pressed lemon oil maintains all the natural compounds of lemon peel, including d-limonene, which gives it superior cleaning power. Many commercial "lemon" cleaners use artificial fragrances and harsh chemicals instead of real lemon oil.

Is your lemon oil product safe for homes with pets?

Yes, when properly diluted and used as directed, our lemon oil is much safer than chemical cleaners. However, we recommend keeping concentrated oils away from pets and ensuring surfaces are dry before allowing pet contact.
