Common questions

Good to know

Is it safe for all surfaces?

Safe for most household surfaces. Test an inconspicuous spot first, and avoid using the concentrate undiluted on unsealed wood or some plastics.

How long does the scent last?

Typically 24–48 hours after application — noticeable without being overwhelming.

Is it pet-safe?

When properly diluted and used as directed, yes. Keep concentrated oil away from pets directly, and let surfaces dry before they make contact.

What's the difference between the spray and the concentrate?

The 24oz spray is pre-mixed with water — open and use. The 16oz concentrate has no added water, so you dilute to whatever strength you need, batch by batch. The concentrate stretches further per dollar and is better for heavier cleaning tasks.
