How To Use Our Lemon Oil

1

Dilute (Concentrate Only)

For the concentrate: Mix 1 ounce with 16 ounces of water in a spray bottle. For tougher jobs, use a stronger concentration. The ready-to-use spray requires no mixing.

2

Apply to Surface

Spray directly onto surfaces or apply with a cloth. For stubborn stains or grime, let the solution sit for 1-2 minutes before wiping.

3

Wipe Clean

Wipe with a clean cloth or paper towel. No need to rinse! Enjoy the fresh, natural lemon scent and sparkling clean results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is your lemon oil safe to use around pets?

Yes, when used as directed, our lemon oil products are safe to use around pets. We recommend allowing cleaned surfaces to dry completely before letting pets have access to them.

What surfaces can I use lemon oil on?

Our lemon oil is versatile and can be used on most hard surfaces including countertops, sinks, bathtubs, tiles, floors, and appliances. We don't recommend using it on unsealed wood or natural stone surfaces without testing in an inconspicuous area first.

How long does the scent last?

The fresh lemon scent typically lasts for several hours after cleaning. Unlike artificial fragrances, our natural lemon oil provides a clean, authentic citrus aroma that isn't overwhelming.

How much concentrate should I use for different cleaning tasks?

For regular cleaning, mix 1 oz of concentrate with 16 oz of water. For tougher cleaning jobs or degreasing, you can use a stronger solution of 1 oz concentrate to 8 oz water. For gentle cleaning of delicate surfaces, dilute further to 1 oz concentrate with 24 oz water.
