Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026. This explains what we collect, why, and what we do with it.

What we collect

If you buy directly on lolemons.com: your name, email, shipping address, and order details. Payment card information is handled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your full card number.

If you sign up for our newsletter: your name (optional) and email address.

If you submit a product review: your name, email, star rating, and review text. Your email is used only to verify you actually purchased the product — it's never shown publicly or shared with anyone outside our team.

How we use it

  • To process and ship your order — your name and address are shared with our fulfillment and shipping partners
  • To process payment, via Stripe
  • To verify a genuine purchase before publishing a product review
  • To send order-related communications (confirmations, shipping updates)
  • To send marketing emails — only if you've separately opted into our newsletter; this is never inferred from a purchase
  • To respond if you contact us, or to follow up if you leave a critical product review

Who we share it with

We use a small number of service providers to run the business, and your information passes through them only as needed to do the above:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Our fulfillment and shipping partners — order fulfillment and delivery
  • Supabase — database hosting
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (order and review-related emails only)

We don't sell your personal information, and we don't share it with anyone for their own marketing purposes.

How long we keep it

Orders placed on lolemons.com are kept as ordinary business records for as long as needed for accounting, warranty, and legal purposes.

Cookies and tracking

We don't run analytics or advertising tracking on this site. Stripe's checkout page (hosted on Stripe's own domain, not ours) may set its own cookies as part of processing payment — that's governed by Stripe's privacy policy, not ours.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in those emails. To request a copy of the information we hold about you, or to ask us to delete it, email contact@lolemons.com and we'll handle it directly — we don't currently have a fully automated self-service tool for this, so requests are handled by a real person on our team.

Children's privacy

This site isn't directed at children, and we don't knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we'll update the date at the top of this page.

Questions?

Email contact@lolemons.com any time.
